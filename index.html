Privacy Policy for Poop Doggo

Last updated: January 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Location data to show nearby bins and your current position
  • Apple ID for authentication purposes
  • User-generated content (bin locations and descriptions)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Display nearby waste bin locations
  • Allow you to add and manage bin locations
  • Authenticate you using Apple Sign In
  • Improve the app's functionality

3. Data Storage

We use Firebase to store:

  • Bin locations and details
  • User authentication information
  • User preferences and favorites

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following services:

  • Firebase (Google) for data storage and authentication
  • Apple Maps for map display
  • Sign in with Apple for authentication
  • Google AdMob for advertisements

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt-out of location services
  • Request your data be removed from our services

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: jacobbeckerbs@gmail.com

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
